<p><strong>The Bus HVAC Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engine Powered HAVC</li><li>Electric Powered HAVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bus HVAC Systems market comprises two primary types: Engine Powered HVAC and Electric Powered HVAC. Engine Powered HVAC systems utilize the bus's internal combustion engine to generate the necessary power for heating and cooling, making them suitable for long-haul journeys where engine efficiency is crucial. In contrast, Electric Powered HVAC systems operate independently from the engine, using electric motors and components to deliver climate control. This type is more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly, particularly in electric or hybrid buses.</p></p>
