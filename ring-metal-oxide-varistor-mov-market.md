<p><p>Ring Metal Oxide Varistors (MOVs) are semiconductor devices used for voltage clamping and surge protection in electrical circuits. Additionally, the rise of electric vehicles and renewable energy sources is further propelling demand, as these applications often deal with high levels of electrical surges.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift toward miniaturization and enhanced performance characteristics in MOVs.
