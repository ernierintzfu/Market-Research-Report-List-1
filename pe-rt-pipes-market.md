<p><p>PE-RT pipes are categorized into two main types: PE-RT I and PE-RT II. PE-RT I offers good flexibility and impact resistance, suitable for various plumbing applications, but may have limitations in high-temperature environments. PE-RT II, on the other hand, is designed for higher thermal stability and enhanced mechanical properties, making it ideal for hot water applications and heating systems. Both types are valued for their resistance to stress cracking and long-term durability, contributing to their growing market demand in construction and infrastructure.</p></p>
