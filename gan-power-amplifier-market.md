<p><p>The GaN power amplifier market primarily includes two types: GaN-on-SiC and GaN-on-Silicon. GaN-on-SiC combines gallium nitride with silicon carbide, offering superior thermal conductivity and efficiency, making it ideal for high-power applications like telecommunications and radar systems. In contrast, GaN-on-Silicon utilizes silicon substrates, providing a more cost-effective solution with moderate performance, suitable for lower power applications such as consumer electronics. Each type caters to specific market needs, balancing performance with cost-efficiency.</p></p>
