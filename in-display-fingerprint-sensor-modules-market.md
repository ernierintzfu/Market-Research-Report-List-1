<p><p>The display fingerprint sensor modules market is primarily categorized into two types: optical fingerprint modules and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Optical fingerprint modules use light to capture fingerprint images, offering high-resolution scans and fast recognition. They are commonly integrated into displays for improved user experience. In contrast, ultrasonic fingerprint modules utilize sound waves to create 3D maps of fingerprints, providing enhanced security and reliability, especially in challenging conditions. Both types are essential in advancing biometric authentication in mobile devices and smart electronics.</p></p>
