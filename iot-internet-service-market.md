<p><p>The IoT Internet Service Market comprises various low-power wide-area network (LPWAN) technologies designed for efficient, long-range communication. Sigfox focuses on ultra-narrowband technology for low-cost, low-data applications, catering to specific industrial and consumer use cases.</p></p>
