<p><p>The magnetorheological damper market is primarily categorized into active and semi-active dampers. Active dampers utilize electromagnetic forces to adjust damping properties in real-time, providing precise control over vehicle dynamics and improving ride comfort. In contrast, semi-active dampers feature adjustable characteristics that respond to external stimuli but do not produce energy independently. These dampers offer a balance between performance and complexity, enhancing safety and stability in automotive applications while being more cost-effective than fully active systems.</p></p>
