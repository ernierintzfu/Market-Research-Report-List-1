<p><strong>The Automotive Servo Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positional Rotation Servo Motors</li><li>Continuous Rotation Servo Motors</li><li>Linear Servo Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive servo motor market comprises three main types: positional rotation servo motors, continuous rotation servo motors, and linear servo motors. Positional rotation servo motors are designed for precise angular movement, ideal for applications like steering and throttle control. Continuous rotation servo motors provide unlimited rotation, useful in robotics and automated systems. Linear servo motors convert electrical energy into linear motion, applying to various automotive applications such as actuators for windows or seats, enhancing automation and efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
