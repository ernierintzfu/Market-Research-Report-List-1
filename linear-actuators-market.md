<p><p>The linear actuators market includes three primary types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air for movement, ideal for high-speed applications and environments requiring cleanliness. Electric actuators rely on electric motors, offering precision and control, making them suitable for automation tasks. Hydraulic actuators use pressurized fluid to generate force, excelling in heavy-duty applications needing significant power and torque. Each type serves specific needs across diverse industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and robotics, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
