<p><p>The acne treatment drug market is divided into two main types: over-the-counter (OTC) and prescription medicines. In contrast, prescription medications are stronger formulations, often prescribed for severe cases or when OTC options fail. Both types aim to reduce acne and improve skin health.</p></p>
