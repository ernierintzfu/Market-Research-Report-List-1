<p><strong>The RFID Transponders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID Transponders</li><li>Active RFID Transponders</li><li>Semi-Active RFID Transponders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID transponder market consists of three main types: passive, active, and semi-active RFID transponders. </p><p>Passive RFID transponders rely on energy from the reader to function, making them cost-effective and widely used for inventory management. Active RFID transponders have their own power source, allowing for longer read ranges and real-time tracking, ideal for asset management. Semi-active RFID transponders combine features of both, using a battery for certain functions while drawing power from the reader, suitable for applications requiring moderate range and frequency.</p></p>
