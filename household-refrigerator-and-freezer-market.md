<p><p>The household refrigerator and freezer market is categorized into three main types: freezer on top, freezer on bottom, and freezer less models. Freezer on top refrigerators feature a freezer compartment at the upper section, providing easy access to frozen items but potentially limiting fresh food space. Freezer on bottom models place the freezer underneath, offering more accessibility to fresh food. Freezer less units lack a freezer compartment altogether, catering to consumers who primarily store fresh foods or require additional freezer space separately.</p></p>
