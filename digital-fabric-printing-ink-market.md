<p><p>The digital fabric printing ink market comprises several types, each suited for specific fabrics and applications. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with fibers, ideal for cotton and other cellulosic materials. Acidic inks are used for protein-based fibers like silk and wool, providing vibrant colors. Paint inks offer versatility on various surfaces, while dispersion inks are designed for synthetic fabrics, ensuring durability and vibrancy. Sublimation inks, ideal for polyester, transfer dye under heat, creating vivid, long-lasting prints.</p></p>
