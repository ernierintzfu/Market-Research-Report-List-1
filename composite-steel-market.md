<p><strong>The Composite Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite steel market includes products created through methods like explosion bonding and roll bonding. Explosion bonding involves using explosive energy to bond two metals, creating a metallurgical bond that offers exceptional strength and durability. Conversely, roll bonding is a mechanical process where two metal sheets are passed through rollers to achieve a bond through pressure and heat. Both methods are crucial for producing advanced materials that cater to industries requiring high-performance, lightweight, and corrosion-resistant solutions.</p></p>
