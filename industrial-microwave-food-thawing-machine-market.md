The continuously type allows for uninterrupted thawing of large quantities as food moves through the microwave chamber, enhancing efficiency. The belt type utilizes a conveyor system, ensuring uniform thawing by exposing items to microwave energy as they progress along the belt. Other variations may include batch systems or smaller units, catering to specific industrial needs or space constraints in food processing facilities.</p></p>
