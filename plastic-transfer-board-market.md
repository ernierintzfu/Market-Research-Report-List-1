<p><p>The plastic transfer board market is categorized into two primary types: with rollers and without rollers. Transfer boards with rollers facilitate smoother movement for patients, reducing friction and minimizing the physical effort required by caregivers during transfers. In contrast, transfer boards without rollers offer a more rigid surface, relying on manual sliding for transfers, which may require more strength but can be more cost-effective. Both types are designed to enhance patient mobility and ensure safety during transfers in healthcare settings.</p></p>
