<p><p>The Titanium Silicon Target market is divided into two main types: Plane Targets and Rotating Targets. Plane Targets consist of flat, stationary surfaces used in various deposition processes, ideal for uniform film thickness in thin-film applications. In contrast, Rotating Targets involve a cylindrical design that allows for continuous rotation during deposition, enhancing material utilization and deposition rates. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as electronics, optics, and solar manufacturing, catering to specific production needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
