<p><p>The adhesives and sealants market comprises various types tailored for different applications. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup, making them ideal for environmentally sensitive uses. Solvent-based adhesives provide strong bonding capabilities but involve volatile organic compounds. Hot-melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials applied in a molten state, suitable for quick bonding and varied substrates. Additionally, other types include reactive adhesives and pressure-sensitive adhesives, addressing specialized needs across industries like construction, automotive, and packaging.</p></p>
