<p><strong>The Photocuring Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free-radical Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photocuring agents market is primarily divided into two types: free-radical and cationic. Free-radical photocuring agents initiate polymerization through the generation of free radicals upon exposure to UV light, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including coatings and adhesives. Conversely, cationic photocuring agents rely on cationic polymerization mechanisms, offering advantages like low odor and better thermal stability, often used in inks and specialty coatings. Both types cater to diverse industries with unique performance characteristics.</p></p>
