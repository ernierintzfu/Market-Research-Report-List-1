<p><ul><li>Condenser Type</li><li>Non-Condenser Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HV bushing market is segmented into two main types: condenser type and non-condenser type. Condenser type bushings incorporate a condenser element, allowing for higher voltage applications while minimizing losses and improving insulation. They are widely used in transformers and GIS systems. Non-condenser type bushings, on the other hand, do not include this feature, making them suitable for lower voltage applications or where space constraints exist. Both types are essential for ensuring safe and efficient energy transmission in electrical infrastructure.</p></p>
