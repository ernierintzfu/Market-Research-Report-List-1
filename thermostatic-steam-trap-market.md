<p><p>The thermostatic steam trap market includes various types, notably the bi-metal strip type and balanced pressure type. The bi-metal strip type relies on a bimetallic element that expands and contracts with temperature changes, efficiently discharging condensate. In contrast, the balanced pressure type utilizes a combination of pressure and temperature for optimal performance, ensuring minimal steam loss while efficiently draining condensate. Both types are essential for maintaining energy efficiency and effective thermal management in steam systems across various industries.</p></p>
