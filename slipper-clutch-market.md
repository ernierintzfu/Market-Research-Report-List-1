<p><p>A slipper clutch is a specialized component used in motorcycles and high-performance vehicles that helps to prevent wheel hop during aggressive downshifting. This technology not only enhances rideability but also improves safety and performance, making it increasingly popular in the automotive market.</p><p>The Slipper Clutch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.00% during the forecast period, driven by rising consumer demand for enhanced vehicle performance and safety features.
