<p><ul><li>Thermally Conductive</li><li>Electrically Conductive</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive elastomer market is categorized into thermally conductive, electrically conductive, and others. Thermally conductive elastomers efficiently transfer heat, making them ideal for applications in electronics and automotive sectors. Electrically conductive elastomers facilitate the flow of electricity, widely used in sensors, connectors, and electromagnetic interference shielding. The 'others' category encompasses specialized formulations, such as piezoresistive and photoconductive elastomers, which serve niche applications in industries like medical devices and robotics, highlighting the versatility of conductive elastomers across various technologies.</p></p>
